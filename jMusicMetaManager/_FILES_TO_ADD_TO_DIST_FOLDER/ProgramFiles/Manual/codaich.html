Codaich

As mentioned in the Introduction, jMusicMetaManager is part of the jMIR project. The Codaich database is a music information retrieval research database consisting of well over 20 000 annotated MP3 recordings. Codaich has been cleaned and profiled using jMusicMetaManager. The metadata for Codaich may be downloaded from the jMIR web page. Features for use in music information retrieval research may be extracted using the OMEN system.

Further information on Codaich and OMEN is available in the following two publications:

McKay, C., D. McEnnis and I. Fujinaga. 2006. A large publicly accessible prototype audio database for music research. Proceedings of the International Conference on Music Information Retrieval. 160–3.

McEnnis, D., C. McKay, and I. Fujinaga. 2006. Overview of OMEN. Proceedings of the International Conference on Music Information Retrieval.7–12.
